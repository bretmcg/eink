## Introduction

E-ink displays have unique characteristics that require careful consideration when designing user interfaces. The high contrast ratio and lack of backlight make them ideal for reading, but the slow refresh rate means animations and rapid updates should be avoided.

## Key Principles

1. **Use pure black and white** - Avoid grays, they dither poorly
2. **Large touch targets** - E-ink screens often have stylus input
3. **Minimal animations** - Each screen refresh is visible
4. **High contrast** - Take advantage of the paper-like display
