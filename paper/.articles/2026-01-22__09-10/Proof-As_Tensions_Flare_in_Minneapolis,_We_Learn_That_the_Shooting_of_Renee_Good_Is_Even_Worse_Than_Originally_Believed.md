### Introduction

Forty-eight hours after the [shooting death](https://abcnews.go.com/US/minneapolis-ice-shooting-minute-minute-timeline-renee-nicole/story?id=129021809) of **Renee Good**, the actions of **ICE** agent **Jonathan Ross**—and his direct and indirect bosses **Donald Trump**, **J.D. Vance**, and **Kristi Noem**—are revealed to have been (and be) more horrifying that we’d realized.

Below are five new developments that transform what was already an unspeakable tragedy—the unjustified killing of a 37-year-old **Minnesota** mother by armed, masked, insignia-less government forces executing an [unprecedentedly unjust](https://www.nilc.org/articles/the-price-of-cruelty-how-trumps-mass-deportation-agenda-endangers-us-all/), [quota-driven](https://www.theguardian.com/us-news/2025/may/29/trump-ice-arrest-quota) mass deportation scheme the likes of which [Trump said he](https://apnews.com/article/fact-check-trump-immigration-crime-ice-criminal-dangerous-violent-99557d9d68642004193a9f4b7668162e) *[wouldn’t](https://apnews.com/article/fact-check-trump-immigration-crime-ice-criminal-dangerous-violent-99557d9d68642004193a9f4b7668162e)* [pursue](https://apnews.com/article/fact-check-trump-immigration-crime-ice-criminal-dangerous-violent-99557d9d68642004193a9f4b7668162e) as he was running for President of the United States back in 2024—into an abiding national emergency that’s [genuinely terrifying](https://news.chapman.edu/2025/10/21/what-americans-fear-most-in-2025-chapman-universitys-annual-survey-reveals-top-fears-and-the-psychology-behind-them/) for those who care about American democracy.
